My take: For up to 3 runs where tear-down is usually on, Option B (slide-up sheet) is likely faster — everything’s visible (no expand step), it’s in the thumb zone, and “Save & add another” lets you bang out all 3 runs without re-tapping +Add. Option A is the lighter touch if you usually add just one run and like seeing the list. Your call.
